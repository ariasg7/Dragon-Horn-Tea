Our Story

Handcrafted Teas, Authentic Flavors, A New York Original

Founded in 2020 by food and business enthusiasts Sean and Cheng, Dragon Tea was born from a shared passion to bring authentic, high-quality bubble tea and snacks to New York. Starting with a single location in the WTC area, we’ve grown to four stores across NYC and Westchester, with more on the way. Known for our handcrafted milk teas, fruit teas, mochi donuts, and fresh-made dumplings, every item we serve is thoughtfully prepared to deliver the best possible experience; natural, fresh, and unforgettable.

Learn More

Our Vision

At Dragon Tea, we believe in redefining what bubble tea can be. Our mission is to serve drinks and snacks that not only taste amazing, but are made with clean, natural ingredients, never artificial flavors or chemicals. We’re proud to support local farms and communities while raising the standard in the tea and snack space. With every expansion, we bring our vision of quality, health-conscious boba culture to more people across the U.S.

Our Services

Simple Ingredients & Real Flavor

Quick Grab & Go

Order ahead through our mobile app or website and skip the wait. Your favorite drinks and snacks are prepared fresh and ready when you arrive — hassle-free convenience.

All-Natural Ingredients

We proudly use real fruit, organic milk, and premium tea leaves. No powders, no shortcuts — just authentic flavors brewed and blended for a clean, refreshing experience every time.

Fresh Mochi Donuts

Our mochi donuts are handmade daily in small batches with rotating flavors like matcha, ube, and strawberry. Perfectly chewy, sweet, and the ultimate pairing with any drink.

Online Ordering & Delivery

Get your cravings delivered straight to your door. Easily place orders online or through delivery apps and enjoy Dragon Tea’s signature drinks and snacks from anywhere in the city.
